All personal data has been replaced with fictional equivalents. The structure, format, and level of detail are authentic.

## How to use these

1. **Browse** to understand what a mature Memex installation looks like
2. **Copy** the structures that fit your domains
3. **Adapt** — replace the fictional data with your real context
4. **Iterate** — your hubs will grow organically as Claude learns your domains
