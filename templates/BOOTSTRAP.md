## What CAN'T be auto-restored

- **userMemories** — auto-generated by the platform. Rebuilds naturally over 1-2 weeks.
- **Conversation history** — gone from platform. Archive files may have summaries.
- **Local tool state** (Apple Notes, Reminders) — local to devices.
- **MCP connector data** — re-accessible after reconnecting.
